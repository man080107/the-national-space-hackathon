Help Page

Welcome to the Help Page! Here you can find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use the Cargo Management System.

How to Add Cargo

To add cargo, navigate to the "Add Cargo" page using the navigation bar. Fill out the form with the required details, such as cargo description, weight, and destination, and click the "Submit" button.

How to Track Shipments

To track shipments, go to the "Track Shipments" section on the homepage. Enter the tracking ID provided at the time of booking to view the current status of your shipment.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@cargo.com.
